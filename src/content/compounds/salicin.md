---
name: "Salicin"
classification: "salicylate glycoside"
summary: "The willow-bark compound metabolized to salicylic acid — the lead behind aspirin."
mechanism: "Converted to salicylic acid, which inhibits COX and reduces prostaglandins."
targets: ["COX","prostaglandins"]
---

Because salicin behaves like a salicylate, willow bark shares aspirin’s cautions: bleeding risk and salicylate allergy.
