---
name: "Monacolin K"
classification: "statin (naturally occurring)"
summary: "The active compound in red yeast rice — chemically identical to the drug lovastatin."
mechanism: "Inhibits HMG-CoA reductase, the rate-limiting enzyme of cholesterol synthesis."
targets: ["HMG-CoA reductase"]
---

Because monacolin K *is* lovastatin, red yeast rice carries the same risks as a statin, with the added problem of unregulated, variable dose and possible citrinin contamination.
