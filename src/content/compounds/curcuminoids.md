---
name: "Curcuminoids"
classification: "polyphenol (curcuminoid)"
summary: "The yellow pigments of turmeric, chiefly curcumin, studied for anti-inflammatory effects."
mechanism: "Inhibit NF-κB and downstream inflammatory cytokines (TNF-α, IL-1β) and the COX-2 / 5-LOX pathways."
targets: ["NF-κB","COX-2","5-LOX","TNF-α","IL-1β"]
---

Curcumin is poorly absorbed on its own, so studied products usually pair it with piperine or use a bioavailability-enhanced formulation.
