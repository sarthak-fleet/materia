---
name: "Muscle soreness (DOMS)"
aliases: ["DOMS","delayed onset muscle soreness","post-exercise soreness"]
summary: "The achy, tender muscles that appear a day or two after unfamiliar or intense exercise."
bodyParts: ["thigh","calf"]
severity: "self-limiting"
redFlags: ["Severe swelling with dark/cola-colored urine after intense exercise (possible rhabdomyolysis)","Pain from a sudden 'pop' or that prevents movement"]

---

DOMS is a normal part of training and resolves on its own. Some recovery aids have modest evidence for easing soreness and restoring strength a little faster.
