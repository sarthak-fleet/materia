The foundation of preventing and treating sarcopenia is **resistance exercise** plus adequate protein —
not a supplement. Within that frame, creatine and protein have the best evidence as *adjuncts* to
training; vitamin D helps mainly in deficiency (and high doses do not prevent falls), and HMB adds
little once you are already exercising.
