Standard care ranges from topical retinoids and benzoyl peroxide to, in more severe cases,
prescription medicines. A few non-prescription options have controlled-trial support as milder
alternatives or adjuncts — topical tea tree oil and topical niacinamide for inflammatory acne, and
oral zinc as a modest add-on.
