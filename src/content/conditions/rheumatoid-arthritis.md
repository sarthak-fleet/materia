---
name: "Rheumatoid arthritis"
aliases: ["RA","inflammatory arthritis"]
summary: "An autoimmune disease causing symmetric joint inflammation, often starting in the hands and wrists."
bodyParts: ["hand"]
severity: "see-a-doctor"
redFlags: ["Persistent symmetric joint swelling and morning stiffness lasting more than 6 weeks (get evaluated early)","A single hot, red, acutely swollen joint with fever","Chest pain, shortness of breath, or new eye redness or vision change"]
order: 3
---

Rheumatoid arthritis needs medical treatment with disease-modifying drugs (DMARDs) to prevent permanent joint damage. Supplements here are adjuncts for symptoms only — never a replacement for that care.
