summary: "Fat build-up in the liver, linked to weight and metabolic health; can progress to inflammation (NASH) and scarring."
bodyParts: ["liver"]
severity: "chronic"
redFlags: ["Yellowing of skin/eyes, abdominal swelling, confusion, or vomiting blood (signs of advanced liver disease)","Easy bruising, marked fatigue, or unexplained weight loss"]

The cornerstone is weight loss and metabolic control. In non-diabetic, biopsy-proven NASH, vitamin E has the best (though imperfect) evidence; omega-3 and milk thistle are weaker.
