name: "Hashimoto’s thyroiditis"
aliases: ["Hashimoto","autoimmune thyroiditis","hypothyroidism"]
summary: "Autoimmune inflammation of the thyroid, the most common cause of an underactive thyroid."
bodyParts: ["thyroid"]
severity: "chronic"
redFlags: ["A rapidly enlarging, hard, or fixed neck lump, or trouble swallowing/breathing","Severe symptoms — very slow heart rate, low body temperature, or confusion"]
order: 40
---

Treatment is thyroid-hormone replacement, guided by blood tests. Selenium lowers thyroid antibody levels in studies, but whether that changes the disease course is unproven.
