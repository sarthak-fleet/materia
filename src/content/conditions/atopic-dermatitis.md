redFlags:

  - "Sudden widespread redness over most of the body (erythroderma) — seek urgent care."
  - "Eczema not controlled despite good moisturising and appropriate over-the-counter steroid use, or severe sleep loss from itching."

The mainstays are consistent moisturising, avoiding triggers, and topical anti-inflammatories.
Oral supplements have been studied but, as the Cochrane review concluded, none is convincingly
effective — which is exactly why the entry below is graded *insufficient* rather than recommended.
