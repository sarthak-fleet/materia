---
name: "High blood pressure"
aliases: ["hypertension","high BP","raised blood pressure"]
summary: "Persistently elevated arterial pressure — usually symptomless, but a major driver of heart attack, stroke, and kidney disease."
bodyParts: ["heart"]
severity: "see-a-doctor"
redFlags: ["BP ≥180/120 with chest pain, breathlessness, severe headache, vision change, or confusion — a hypertensive emergency (call emergency services)","Sudden \"thunderclap\" headache or signs of stroke (face droop, arm weakness, speech trouble)"]
order: 30
---

Diet (DASH, less salt), exercise, weight, and prescribed medication are the foundation. Several supplements lower BP by a few mmHg — useful adjuncts, not replacements for treatment.
