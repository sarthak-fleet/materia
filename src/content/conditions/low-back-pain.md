aliases: ["lower back pain","lumbago","backache"]
summary: "Pain in the lumbar spine, usually non-specific and self-limiting, but occasionally a sign of something serious."
bodyParts: ["lower-back"]
severity: "self-limiting"
redFlags: ["Loss of bladder or bowel control, or numbness around the groin/saddle area","Progressive leg weakness or numbness","Fever, or a history of cancer, with new back pain","Significant trauma, or minor trauma if you have osteoporosis","Unexplained weight loss or pain that is worse at night"]
order: 2
---

Most low back pain improves within weeks with movement and time. A few herbal remedies have short-term evidence for symptom relief. The red flags above point to causes that need a clinician rather than self-treatment.
