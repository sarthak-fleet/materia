For small, shallow burns the priorities are cooling with running water, gentle cleaning, and
covering. Topical aloe vera has trial evidence for speeding healing of minor (second-degree) burns;
it is an adjunct to, not a substitute for, proper first aid and medical care when a burn is serious.
