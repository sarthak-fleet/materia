Depression is a medical condition that deserves professional care; the options below are studied
mostly in **mild-to-moderate** depression and are not a substitute for diagnosis or treatment. Two of
them — St John's wort and SAMe — are serotonergic and carry real interaction risks, so they should
never be combined with prescription antidepressants without a clinician.
