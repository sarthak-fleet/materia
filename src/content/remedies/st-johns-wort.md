St John's wort genuinely works for mild-to-moderate depression — but it **induces drug-metabolising
enzymes**, silently lowering the levels of many essential medicines (including the contraceptive pill
and transplant drugs) and raising serotonin-syndrome risk with antidepressants. Anyone taking other
medication should treat it as a drug, not a gentle herb, and tell their prescriber.
