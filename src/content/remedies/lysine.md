Lysine is thought to counter the arginine that herpes virus needs to replicate. The trials disagree —
some show fewer cold-sore recurrences at ~1 g/day, others (and Cochrane) show nothing — so it is a
low-risk option of uncertain benefit, not a proven treatment.
