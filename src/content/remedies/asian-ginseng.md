Asian ginseng is among the most famous tonic herbs, but the controlled evidence is underwhelming —
at most a small memory effect, and disagreement between reviews on fatigue. It can disturb sleep and
lower blood sugar, so time doses earlier and watch for interactions with diabetes drugs.
