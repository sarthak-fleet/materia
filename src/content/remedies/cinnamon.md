Culinary amounts of cinnamon are fine. For blood sugar, the effect is small and unreliable, and the
cheap "cassia" type contains coumarin, which can stress the liver at high supplemental doses — Ceylon
cinnamon is lower in coumarin. It is not a substitute for diabetes treatment.
