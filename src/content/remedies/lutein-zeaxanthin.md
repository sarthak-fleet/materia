Lutein and zeaxanthin are the carotenoids that concentrate in the macula. On their own they don't
dramatically slow AMD, but they're the safe, evidence-based replacement for beta-carotene in the
AREDS2 formula — equally or more effective and without the smoker lung-cancer risk.
