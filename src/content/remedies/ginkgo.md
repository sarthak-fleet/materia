Despite its popularity, ginkgo is a cautionary tale: the largest, longest prevention trial (GEM, >3,000
elderly over ~6 years) found no benefit, and the Cochrane treatment review called the evidence
unreliable. Meanwhile it antagonises platelet function and carries a real bleeding risk.
